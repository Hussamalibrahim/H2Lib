Your data helps us:

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls to personal information

4. Third-Party Services

We may use these trusted services:

  • Google Analytics for usage statistics (anonymous data)
  • Email service providers for notifications

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask for corrections to inaccurate information
  • Delete your account (except records required by law)

Contact admin@admin.com to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify users of significant changes via email or system notice.
